How to choose your new cat

When you are thinking about getting a cat, there are multiple things need to consider first. One would include whether you want your cat to be an indoor cat, outdoor cat, or both. Some cats aren't suited well for an outdoor environment. You will also need to consider what other pets you have, such as dogs, other cats, or small pets like hamsters or guinea pigs. Some cats won't get along well with other pets. You will also want to figure out whether you want a energetic, talkative cat or if you would want quiet, calm cat. Some breeds are very energetic and can be very loud, other breeds can be very quiet and affectionate. These are important things to think about when you are getting your new cat.
